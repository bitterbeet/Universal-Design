About Me

Hello, my name is Amelia! I’m a senior majoring in CTD with a focus on creative digital design. I love exploring how technology and art intersect to tell meaningful stories and shape the ways we communicate online. My work often blends visual design, accessibility, and digital media experimentation.

Outside of class, I enjoy hiking, painting, and spending time with my cats. I’m passionate about building digital spaces that are inclusive, usable, and welcoming for everyone. Through my projects, I hope to continue learning how to design with empathy and create experiences that bring people together.
